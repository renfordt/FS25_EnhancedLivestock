## Reproduction

### Fertility
Pigs reach sexual maturity at approximately 6-8 months of age.

### Pregnancy
- **Gestation period:** Approximately 4 months
- **Litter size:** 6-14 piglets
- **Minimum health for breeding:** 75%

!!! info "Large Litters"
    Pigs have the largest litter sizes of any livestock in the game. Plan your housing capacity accordingly.

## Common Diseases

- [Foot and Mouth](foot_and_mouth.md) - Highly contagious
- [Porcine Epidemic Diarrhea (PED)](porcine_epidemic_diarrhea.md) - Especially dangerous for piglets

## Pig Management Tips

!!! tip "Successful Pig Farming"
    - **Housing capacity** - Plan for large litter sizes
    - **Separate boars** - Keep breeding boars separate from market pigs
    - **Early castration** - Castrate male piglets not intended for breeding
    - **Monitor PED** - Young piglets are extremely vulnerable to PED
    - **Quality genetics** - Focus on quality trait for premium meat prices

## Economic Considerations

### Strengths
- Fast reproduction cycle
- Large litter sizes
- Quick growth to market weight
- Efficient feed conversion

### Challenges
- Housing costs for large numbers
- Disease management with high populations
- Need for proper manure management
